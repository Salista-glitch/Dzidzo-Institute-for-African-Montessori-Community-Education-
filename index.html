MoW Institute for African Montessori & Community Education

Rooted in Ubuntu.
Reimagining Montessori.

MoW Institute for African Montessori & Community Education is a teacher training and leadership development center dedicated to reimagining Montessori education for African classrooms. Rooted in Ubuntu and powered by Montessori on Wheels (MoW), the Institute prepares educators to deliver child-centered, culturally grounded, and community-responsive learning in both urban and rural contexts.

We exist to close the gap between Montessori theory and the lived realities of African schools. Our training integrates Montessori principles with:

  • • Multilingual classrooms
  • • Large class sizes
  • • Competency-based curriculum demands
  • • Low-resource environments

Through practical workshops, coaching, research, and community partnership, we equip teachers not only to manage classrooms—but to transform them.

Our Purpose

Vision & Mission

Our vision is an Africa where every classroom nurtures dignity, curiosity, and leadership, guided by Montessori principles and grounded in Ubuntu.
Our mission is to prepare and support African educators to deliver Montessori education that is culturally responsive, community-rooted, and excellence-driven.

Core Commitments

  • • Child-centered excellence
  • • Community partnership
  • • Cultural identity and heritage integration
  • • Professional dignity for teachers
  • • Practical innovation for low-resource environments

"The Institute is more than a training center. It is a professional home for educators who believe that every child carries brilliance, and that education must honor identity, heritage, and dignity."
